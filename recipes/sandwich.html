Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

Spicy Mayoli

  • 1/2 cup (89g) mayonnaise
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 tablespoons (44g) sriracha
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper to taste

Filling

  • Spicy Mayoli
  • 4 slices of gouda cheese
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 12 good slices mortadella, seared
  • 4 large eggs, fried
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
